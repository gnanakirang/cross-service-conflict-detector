# Demo scenarios (recording script)

Scenario A — Liquidity conflict (3 minutes)
1. Open Dashboard: show profile values (balance, EMI, SIP).
2. Click Analyze: show detected liquidity conflict and AI explanation.
3. Narration: explain why EMI + SIP > balance and show suggested actions (Pause SIP, shift funds, reschedule EMI).

Scenario B — Timing conflict (3 minutes)
1. Use transaction simulator to stream a near-term expense without incoming salary.
2. Show ConflictView updating and AI recommending short-term mitigation (emergency fund, short-term transfer).
3. End with "future work" slide: automated execution with consent, refinancing integration, richer heuristics.
